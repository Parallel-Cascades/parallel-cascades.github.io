About

Parallel Cascade is the website of Emil Tonev, a video game developer based in Sofia, Bulgaria. Here you can find projects I'm currently working on - data-oriented systems for Unity's ECS, procedural shaders and visual effects and more.

In the future, some of the completed systems and asset packages listed will be published on the Unity Asset Store. Also stay tuned for the announcement of Titanomachia, a space game utilising the power of DOTS and ECS to deliver chaotic destruction on a galactic scale.
